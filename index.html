Introduction

Telecommunication systems in Papua New Guinea are highly exposed to lightning, power instability, and generator switching surges. These disturbances damage sensitive RF and power electronics, causing downtime and equipment failure.

Objectives

  • Reduce surge damage on telecom equipment
  • Improve grounding and earthing systems
  • Analyze overvoltage and transient spikes
  • Increase system lifetime reliability

Methodology

  • Laboratory testing (PNGUoT Electrical Lab)
  • Field testing at Vodafone PNG sites
  • Simulation (MATLAB, Proteus, Arduino)
  • Data logging & waveform analysis
