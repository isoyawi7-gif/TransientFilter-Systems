Introduction

Telecommunications equipment in Papua New Guinea is frequently affected by power instability, blackouts, and lightning strikes. During transitions between power sources, surge currents damage sensitive components, causing failures and downtime.

This research focuses on improving surge protection, grounding, and earthing systems to enhance equipment reliability and reduce operational risks.

Research Objectives

  • Mitigate equipment stalling during steady-state operation
  • Improve surge protection and earthing systems
  • Capture and analyze overcurrent events
  • Ensure long-term system reliability

Methodology
